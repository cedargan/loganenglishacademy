Programs

Six levels, one clear path

Classes are named for the character we hope to build. Students advance from first words and phonics all the way to reading classic English novels.

Kind

Level 1 · Foundations

First vocabulary, phonics, and everyday English through songs, stories, and picture dictionaries.

Brave

Level 2 · Early Readers

Beginning readers start their first story books with audio support, plus core grammar and writing practice.

Truth

Level 3 · Developing

Growing readers expand grammar and vocabulary while reading non-fiction and fiction readers every week.

Virtue

Level 4 · Intermediate

Students strengthen academic vocabulary and writing, working through longer texts and word-study books.

Patience

Level 5 · Advanced

Confident readers take on chapter books and novel studies with reading diaries, reviews, and discussion.

Honor

Level 6 · Mastery

Our highest level reads classic English literature and tackles advanced grammar, writing, and presentations.
